Mission

Our Development Center focuses on helping children and adults to improve their lives, by providing job skills training, leadership, life skill classes, and financial literacy training. We will work with and partner with local agencies willing to offer apprenticeship and internship opportunities. Liberty Development Center believes everyone deserves an opportunity and our goal is to provide meaningful support to the homeless community and our brave veterans as well.

Vision

We will provide diversified services based on the needs of the community by being responsive to the community and to anticipate and seek out the community's needs with the intent to improve outcomes daily.
